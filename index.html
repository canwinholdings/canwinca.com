We Are

CanWin

CanWin is a Toronto-based innovation business accelerator, helping high-potential startups with product development and product-market fit to grow their companies in Canada. We provide a full range of business development services including early-stage fund-raising, technical skills development, risk management and equity planning with support from our experienced and knowledgeable team in Canada and China.

With strong market networks in Beijing, Shanghai, Hong Kong and Chengdu, our reputable and experienced team at CanWin attract talented individuals and innovative projects in the fields of information technology, IoT, artificial intelligence, Blockchain, smart city and environmental technology.
